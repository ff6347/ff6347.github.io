In the recent project of our master studio we worked on the topic "ethics of interaction". The task was based on the [IPPSO project](https://uclab.fh-potsdam.de/ippso/)

I'd like to have some discourse about designing for technology that seems to do more harm than good. I know this can be applied to a lot of things, but in the context of the smart glasses there are so many dystopian ideas coming up that I'm really struggling with making interfaces that look good and are usable when my ideas can be used as the basis for something that will make people suffer. 

I guess using default iOS, liquid glass or material UI is an escape hatch for that conundrum. When just using the default, we can focus on what this technology creates and implicates rather than developing something usable.
